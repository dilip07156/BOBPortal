Payment of your Credit Card bill was never this easy! Come and experience it.

  • Pay your credit card bill
  • Instantly through Insta-Pay using your net banking [supported by 50 + banks in India] without logging in Bobcard’s Portal.

Experience the convenience of easy seamless payment through the following simple steps:

  • Click on the link Pay-Now.
  • Enter your Credit card [Bobcard] Number and renter the same to confirm.
  • Enter CAPTCHA.
  • Proceed and Confirm to verify card holder’s Name.
  • Enter Amount you wish to pay, email id and your mobile number.
  • Choose any of the Bank for initiating payment [Option to use "Bank of Baroda Net-banking” or "Other Bank Net-Banking”].
  • Follow the Netbanking payment process and upon successful completion of transaction you will receive the payment confirmation and receipt for the same.Payment will reflect in your credit card account within two (2) working days.
